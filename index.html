FAQ

Common Questions

Do you offer online or in-person sessions?
Both. In-person sessions are available in Dubai. Online sessions are available for families anywhere in the world — particularly useful for school consulting for families relocating to Dubai.
What age groups do you work with?
Children aged 3.5–12 for tutoring and school readiness. School consulting and academic advisory sessions are for parents of children of any school age.
How do sessions work for young children?
Sessions are structured but playful, tailored to short attention spans and natural curiosity. Each child gets a personalised learning plan, and progress is shared with parents regularly — typically 45–60 minutes per session.
Which Dubai schools are you familiar with?
I have broad knowledge across the full range of Dubai's private school sector — British, American, IB, Indian, and more — and stay current with KHDA inspection reports, admissions timelines, and fee structures.
Do you have affiliations with any schools?
None. I accept no referral fees or commissions from schools. My advice is entirely independent and based solely on what's right for your child and family.
Do you offer packages or one-off sessions?
Both are available. For tutoring, regular weekly sessions consistently produce the best results. For consulting, many families find a single structured session gives them all the clarity they need.
