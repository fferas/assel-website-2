FAQ

Common Questions

Do you offer online or in-person sessions?
Both. In-person sessions are available in Dubai. Online sessions are available for families anywhere in the world — particularly useful for families relocating to Dubai.
What age groups do you work with?
Children aged 3.5–18 for tutoring. School consulting and academic advisory are for parents of children of any school age.
How do sessions work for young children?
Sessions are structured but playful, tailored to short attention spans. Each child gets a personalised learning plan — typically 45–60 minutes. Progress is shared with parents regularly.
Which Dubai schools are you familiar with?
I have broad knowledge across Dubai's full private school sector — British, American, IB, Indian, and more — and stay current with KHDA inspection reports.
Do you have affiliations with any schools?
None. I accept no referral fees or commissions. My advice is entirely independent and based solely on what's right for your child.
Do you offer packages or one-off sessions?
Both. Regular weekly sessions produce the best results for tutoring. For consulting, many families find a single structured session gives them all the clarity they need.
